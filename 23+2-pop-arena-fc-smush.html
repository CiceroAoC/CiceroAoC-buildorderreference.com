23+2 pop FC - Smush

Siege and monks - a classic, aggressive arena build. Best with civs that have a good monastery - atonement is important vs civs which have it. This is an 'all-in' version of the build which requires selling stone in Feudal, and then some more market abuse in Castle - buying food is preferable to buying wood, as food is slower to gather and requires a wood investment. Saracens can pull off a special version of the build due to their market bonus - for them, mining/selling stone is better than mining gold.
