Script

cic-14-01 Build a house with one villager, and all your villagers gathering from sheep as quickly as possible. Research loom.
cic-14-02 You will now need to force drop with your villagers under the town center to keep the town center working. Send the next villager to sheep as well.
cic-14-03 The next 5 villagers should go to berries. Build 1 house with the next villager.
cic-14-04 Now move more villagers to wood, so that you have 11 villagers on wood, and also move 1 more villager to build a farm. The rest of this build is up to you now so good luck! [or something like this]


cic-80-00 You will need to use deer for this build order - use your scout to push them under the town center.
cic-80-10 Send the next 3 villagers to gather food under the town center, and start moving villagers to farms.
cic-80-20 Send the next 2 villagers to wood.


cic-81-01 Send the next 4 villagers to wood and build a new lumbercamp.
cic-81-02 Keep sending villagers to farms until you have 4 farms.
cic-81-03 Now research both double-bit axe and horse collar. As the sheep finish, move 2 more villagers to gold, and have 4 villagers take stragglers close to the town center.
cic-81-04 You can now buy 100 food.
cic-81-05 Now start moving the 4 villagers from stragglers to a nearby woodline where you want to build a town center. You will also build a town center with the villagers on gold.
cic-81-06 Immediately place 2 town centers, and research bow saw when you can. You should then start adding farms every time you have 60 wood.
cic-81-07 You can keep some villagers on gold so you can buy more food as needed to keep your town centers running.
cic-81-08 At about 25 farms you can start sending more villagers to gold. Keep sending villagers to farms.
cic-81-09 Now you will soon need to build a university and monastery.


cic-82-01 The next villager should mine 20g before you send it to wood.
cic-82-02 Keep moving villagers to farms until you have 6 farms.
cic-82-03 Now sell 100 stone and click up to Castle.
cic-82-04 Now research both double-bit axe and horse collar. As the sheep finish, move your 4 villagers to take stragglers close to the town center.
cic-81-05 Now start moving 2 villagers from stragglers to a nearby woodline where you want to build a town center. Move the other 2 villagers from stragglers to one of your golds. You will also build a town center with the villagers on stone.
cic-81-06 Immediately place 3 town centers, and research bow saw when you can. You should then start adding farms every time you have 60 wood.
cic-81-07 Once you start banking food, you should start researching more eco upgrades like hand cart, heavy plow and gold mining.
cic-81-08 At about 30 farms you can start sending more villagers to gold. Keep sending villagers to farms.


cic-82-01 Send the next 3 villagers to gather food under the town center, and start moving villagers to farms until you have 3 farms.
cic-82-02 Now send the next 3 villagers to stone.
cic-82-03 Now research both double-bit axe and horse collar. Move 2 more villagers to wood from sheep, then start moving villagers from around the town center to make more farms.
cic-82-04 Once the rest of the villagers from sheep have gone to farms, you can start moving villagers from berries to farms as well.
cic-82-05 Now build a castle using all of the villagers from stone. Research bow saw, and send the next villagers to farms and gold as needed.
cic-82-06 Now start making conquistadors.


cic-83-01 Now build a stable and a blacksmith and send the next 2 villagers to wood. You should then be able to click up to the Castle age.
cic-83-02 Now build a monastery, and then place your a 2nd town center with about 4 villagers. Then research bow saw, and start moving villagers from berries to farms.
cic-83-03 Now start making monks and collecting relics. Research wheelbarrow and continue moving berry villagers to farms.
cic-83-04 You should now look to build you're 3rd town center as soon as you are able.
cic-83-05 Don't forget to build a university so you can click up.


cic-84-01 The next 3 villagers should go to berries. Build 2 houses with the next villager.
cic-84-02 Now send the next 5 villagers to gold.
cic-84-03 Now the last 4 villagers should go to stone.
cic-84-04 Now move more villagers to farms. You will eventually need 10 farms in total once your sheep have run out.
cic-84-05 Now build a market and a blacksmith. Send 1 more villager to gold, and then 1 more villager to stone. You should then be able to click up to the Castle age.
cic-84-06 Now research both double-bit axe and gold mining.
cic-84-07 Now build a castle using all of the villagers from stone. Send the next villagers to gold, and build a second mining camp.
cic-84-08 Start building monasteries, until you have a total of three monasteries.
cic-84-09 Now buy food as needed so you can click up to Imperial.
cic-84-10 Now reduce the number of villagers on food so you have only 6 on farms. Send the rest to gold, and also send the villagers from stone to gold. Start making monks, and make also 2 petards. Research Sancity.
cic-84-11 Now start making treb(uchet)s, and research both block printing and illumination.
