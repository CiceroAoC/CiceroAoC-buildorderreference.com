cic-50-12 Now send more villagers to wood, until you have 10 villagers split between 2 lumber camps.

cic-70-01 Click Feudal with 21 population.
cic-70-02 Research loom, and then click Feudal with 21 population.
cic-70-04 The gold villager should now gather food under the town center.
cic-70-06 Now move more villagers to wood so that you have 11 villagers split between 2 lumber camps. Also move 4 villagers to gold. The remaining 2 villagers should chop stragglers.


cic-71-00 Now click Feudal with 24 population.
cic-71-01 Now click Feudal with 25 population.
cic-71-02 Now click Feudal with 26 population.
cic-71-03 Now research loom, and then click Feudal with 26 population.
cic-71-04 Now click Feudal with 29 population.


cic-72-01 Send the next villager to wood.
cic-72-02 Send 3 villagers to build a second lumber camp, so that you have 8 on wood. Now start making 3 militia.
cic-72-03 Also make 2 farms using villagers from sheep.
cic-72-04 Now immediately research both double-bit axe and men-at-arms, and build an archery range as soon as you have the wood. Send the next villagers to gold, until you have 5 on gold.
cic-72-05 Move 2 more villagers from food to wood, then start making more farms around the town center with the villagers from sheep.
cic-72-06 Build a blacksmith once you have about 6 farms.
cic-72-07 Now start adding more villagers to gold, so you have 8 on gold, and build a second archery range.


cic-73-01 Use the next villager to start walling your base.
cic-73-02 Now start making 3 militia. Build another house, then use the villager that built the barracks to help wall your base. Start moving villagers from under the town center to make new farms, every time you have 60 wood in the bank.
cic-73-03 You should pull villagers from resources to help wall your base if needed.
cic-73-04 Now move 4 villagers to gold. Once you are fully walled and the sheep are finished, you should add 2 more villagers to wood and 2 more villagers to berries.
cic-73-05 Now move villagers from berries to gold, so you have about 9 on gold, and research double-bit axe. Start making archers as soon as you can, and build a second range once you have the wood for it.
cic-73-06 Remember to also research fletching before you reach Castle.
cic-73-07 Now research crossbowmen, followed by bodkin arrow as soon as you can afford it. Keep making archers.


cic-80-00 You will need to make use of the deer during this build order - use your scout to push them under the town center.
cic-80-01 Send the next 3 villagers to gather food under the town center.
cic-80-02 Also start moving villagers from the boar to build farms.
cic-80-03 Send the next 2 villagers to wood.
cic-80-04 Send the next 4 villagers to wood and build a new lumbercamp.
cic-80-05 Now research both double-bit axe and horse collar. As the sheep run out, move 2 more villagers to wood, and then start adding more farms with the villagers under the town center.
cic-80-06 Now build a university and a monastery.
cic-80-07 Don't forget to build another house.


cic-81-01 Keep sending villagers to farms until you have 4 farms.
cic-81-02 Now research both double-bit axe and horse collar. As you run out of food under the town center, move 2 more villagers to gold, and have the remaining 4 villagers take stragglers close to the town center.
cic-81-03 Now start moving the 4 villagers from stragglers to a nearby woodline where you want to build a town center. You will also build a town center with the villagers on gold.
cic-81-04 Immediately place 2 town centers, and research bow saw. You should then start adding farms every time you have 60 wood.
cic-81-05 Now produce villagers from all 3 town centers. Keep some villagers on gold so you can buy food as needed to keep your town centers running.
cic-81-06 Once you have about 25 farms you can research gold mining and start sending more villagers to gold. Keep adding more villagers to farms.


cic-82-01 Mine 20 gold with the next villager before you send it to wood.
cic-82-02 Keep moving villagers to farms until you have 6 farms.
cic-82-03 Now build a market and a blacksmith and send the next 2 villagers to wood. You will then need to sell 100 stone to click up to Castle.
cic-82-04 Now research both double-bit axe and horse collar. As the sheep finish, move your 4 villagers to take stragglers close to the town center.
cic-82-05 Now start moving 2 villagers from stragglers to a nearby woodline where you want to build a town center. Move the other 2 villagers from stragglers to one of your gold spots. You should also build a town center with the villagers on stone.
cic-82-06 Immediately place 3 town centers, and research bow saw. You should then start adding farms every time you have 60 wood.
cic-82-07 Once you have about 30 farms, you should be able to start getting more eco upgrades like hand cart, heavy plow and gold mining. Start sending more villagers to gold in addition to farms.


cic-83-01 Now build a stable and a blacksmith and send the next 2 villagers to wood. You should then be able to click up to the Castle age.
cic-83-02 Once the villagers from sheep have gone to farms, you can also move 2 villagers from berries to make farms.
cic-83-03 Now build a monastery, and place your a 2nd town center with about 4 villagers. Research bow saw, and send the next 2 villagers to gold.
cic-83-04 Now research wheelbarrow. Start making monks to collect the relics.
cic-83-05 Build your third town center as soon as you are able, and then start adding farms every time you have 60 wood.
cic-83-06 Don't forget to build a university so you can click up.


cic-84-01 Also start moving villagers to farms, until you have 3 farms.
cic-84-02 Now send the next 3 villagers to stone.
cic-84-03 Now start making conquistadors. The villagers that built the Castle can go to gold.


cic-85-01 Send the next 4 villagers to gather food under the town center, and start moving villagers to farms.
cic-85-02 Now send the last 5 villagers to gold.
cic-85-03 Now keep moving more villagers to farms, until you have 8 farms.
cic-85-04 Now build a market and a blacksmith and send the next 2 villagers to gold. You should then be able to click up to the Castle age.
cic-85-05 Now research both double-bit axe and gold mining, and sell your 200 stone.
cic-85-06 Once the sheep have run out, send these villagers to build a second mining camp on gold.
cic-85-07 As the berries finish, move these villagers to wood.
cic-85-08 Now build a siege workshop and monastery, and send the next 2 villagers to gold. You will then need to buy food to click up to Imperial.
cic-85-09 Now build a barracks, followed by 2 archery ranges.
cic-85-10 Now start making hand cannoneers and bombard cannons.


cic-86-01 Build one house, and get all of your villagers gathering from sheep as quickly as possible. Research loom.
cic-86-02 You will now need to force drop with your villagers under the town center to keep the town center working. Send the next villager to sheep, so that you have 7 on sheep.
cic-86-03 The next 5 villagers should go to berries. Build 1 house with the next villager.
cic-86-04 Make 1 farm using one of the villagers from the boar, and remember to build another house.
cic-86-05 Now move more villagers to wood, so that you have 11 villagers on wood. You can also make 1 more farm at this point. The rest of this build should be familiar to you.


cic-87-01 Build a house with the next villager, then send the villager to wood, so you have 4 on wood.
cic-87-02 The next 5 villagers should go to berries.
cic-87-03 The next villager should go to boar. Remember to build another house.
cic-87-04 Now move more villagers to wood, so that you have 12 villagers on wood, and also move 3 villagers to gold. The rest of this build should be familiar to you.


cic-90-01 Build a house, and then a dock with the next villager.
cic-90-02 Now start making 4 fishing ships and have them collect deep fish. Build another house with the dock villager, then have this villager collect shore fish until you need to build more buildings.
cic-90-03 Now send the rest to gather food under the town center. Remember to lure the second boar.
cic-90-04 Now move all your villagers to wood. Build a second lumbercamp as soon as you are able, and have 7 villagers assigned to each one. The rest of your villagers should chop stragglers around the town center.


cic-91-01 Now move 6 villagers from stragglers to gold.
cic-91-02 Now build a second dock before you reach Feudal.
cic-91-03 Now start making fire galleys from both docks and research double-bit axe. Build a third dock as soon as you have the wood. Your next villagers should go to sheep and berries.
cic-91-04 Now produce fire galleys from all 3 docks.
cic-91-05 Once you have about 6 villagers on berries you can start making farms.
cic-91-06 Now make a market and a blacksmith.


cic-92-01 Now move 4 villagers from stragglers to gold.
cic-92-02 Now research double-bit axe, and start producing some fire galleys. Build 2 ranges as soon as you are able, and send the next villagers to gold, until you have 8 on gold.
cic-92-03 Now start making archers. Once you have 2 ranges you should then build a blacksmith for fletching.
cic-92-04 Your next villagers should go to sheep and berries. Move villagers from wood to food, until you have only 10 on wood.
cic-92-05 Once you have about 6 villagers on berries you can start making farms.
