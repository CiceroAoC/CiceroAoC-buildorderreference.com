21 pop Archers [pick civ]

Here is a selection of suggestions for civilizations which can pull off a 21 pop archer build. It is certainly not necessary to know all these builds, but it can be nice to see how you could adjust your build to take advantage of an eco bonus. Also just because you can advance earlier with these civs, it doesn't mean you have to - you might want to put the extra resources into horse collar instead for example.

The scenario for these builds allows you to pick your civ - the following civs are recommended: Celts, Ethiopians, Japanese, Khmer, Lithuanians & Malians. Mongols are also recommended for the build, but will struggle to get an A+ for the final benchmark.
