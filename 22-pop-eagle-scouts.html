22 pop Eagle Scouts

Straight eagle scouts; allows constant production from two barracks upon reaching Feudal. Requires both blacksmith upgrades early on for them to be effective. Usually not viable against experienced opponents, with men-at-arms - eagles being a better option. More commonly, eagle scouts would begin massing in late Feudal so there is an army ready to upgrade upon reaching Castle.
