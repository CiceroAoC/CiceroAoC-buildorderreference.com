31+2+4 pop FI - monks + trebs

Good strategy for flanks in team games for civs with a strong monastery. Below build is optimised for the scenario requirements which is very gold-heavy, but you will want more on wood/farms if adding pikes for example.

The scenario for these builds uses Magyars because they are completely generic here, but they are not recommended for this strategy due to their lack of redemption!
