27+2 Arena Scouts + Monks

An important strategy for 1v1 arena. Scouts are made to pick off enemy monks, so the player can secure the relics for themselves. This version has you transition into a boom, but more aggression options are also viable.

When generating new maps, the relic placement may make things more difficult, but it should be possible to collect all relics in time by placing the monastery further forward.
