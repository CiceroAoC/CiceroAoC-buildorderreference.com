28+2 pop FC - Unique Unit

Fast Castle and have the stone to build one when you reach it. Sometimes used from pocket with civs such as Mayans/Spanish/Burmese. This build is perhaps less important than the others in this section, but it is a straightforward build to execute and is placed here in the series to give you more practice at fast-castling. It is especially worth knowing for Mayan pocket since they don't get knights.
